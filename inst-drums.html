BIO

STELLA KOULOUKOUSA

Stella Kouloukousa was born in Athens, Greece in 1993.

At the age of 14 she started taking drum lessons and since then, drumming is a huge part of her life.

In 2011 she started her music career as a session drummer performing in various of places in Athens and in festivals. During the period 2011-2014, she obtained music diplomas such as: Grades 4,6,7 and 8 in drums and Grades 5 and 6 in music theory by the London College of Music and Multimedia. In 2014, she expanded her knowledge by enrolling in the Music Department of the University of Nicosia (Cyprus). Since then, she has been an active performer, having the chance to collaborate with some of the country's well known and respected musicians, as well as experimenting with various of genres (rok, pop, jazz, latin, ethnic jazz and traditional Greek music).

After she graduated with honors (cum laude) in 2018, she started teaching drums in private music schools, while still performing around the island.
