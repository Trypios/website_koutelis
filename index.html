LESSONS

Available music lessons:

Lessons are available for all ages and abilities (beginner to advanced) and cover many music styles (rock, pop, metal, alternative, fusion, funk, classical etc). Emphasize will be given on the style the student favors the most and his/her personal goals. We offer the following lessons:

  • Guitar [Electric, Acoustic, Classical, Bass]
  • Piano [Modern, Classical]
  • Drums
  • Vocals
  • Wind/Brass instruments
  • Music theory
  • Music technology [Recording, Cubase, Sibelius]
  • and much more, ask for availability

Exams:

It is up to the student if they want to take exams. Any music examination is a proper motivational tool to force a little bit more practicing time and dedication. It is not necessary for all people, yet always beneficial. The international graded music exams we prepare the students for are:

  • Rockschool
  • Trinity College London
  • ABRSM – Associated Board of the Royal Schools of Music, London
