BIO

EVRIDIKI MALLOURI

Evridiki Mallouri was born in Nicosia, Cyprus in 1992.

She started taking piano lessons at a very young age and since then, music became part of her life.

In 2010 she enrolled in a music BA at the University of Nicosia, focusing on piano and vocals. She earned her degree in 2014, awarded as Top graduate in music education concentration. The following year (2015) the studies continued, obtaining a MA in Music Pedagogy and since then has been an active piano tutor.

Evridiki is currently studying English Language and Literature at the European University, expecting her BA in 2021.
